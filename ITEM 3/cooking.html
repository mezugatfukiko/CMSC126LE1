Kinilaw Cooking Guide

Follow these steps to prepare delicious Kinilaw.

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

Gather all ingredients, including fresh yellowfin tuna, vinegar, onion, ginger, calamansi, salt, and pepper.

Next

Step 2: Wash the Tuna

Combine tuna cubes with 3/4 cup vinegar in a bowl. Let sit for 2 minutes, then drain while slightly squeezing out excess vinegar.

Back Next

Step 3: Mix Ingredients

Combine the drained tuna with red onion, ginger, calamansi, salt, and pepper. Toss gently until well blended.

Back Next

Step 4: Chill the Kinilaw

Cover the bowl and place it inside the fridge for at least 30 minutes but no longer than 3 hours.

Back Next

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

Serve chilled and enjoy with a pairing of beer!
